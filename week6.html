Ideas for the final project

Interdisciplinary Project Winter Semester 2018/2019 Rhine-Waal University of Applied Sciences FabLab Kamp-Lintfort by Adriana Cabrera, Prof. Karsten Nebe, Prof. William Megill Faculty Enviroment and Energy

Week 6 consisted basically of the repetition of the laser cutter since most of the students did not finish their project before class.


In the end and as a homework we were supposed to think of ideas for our final projects.


Here are my ideas:

  • Innovative version of the Kleinert Gips
  • Corsette for the lower belly, that warms up when women have their period and are in pain. Maybe it could even have a massage function
  • Walking stick that has an integrated navigation system or sos button, or storage for pills that need to be taken daily
  • Glasses with integrated flash light, that recognize colors (for colorblind)
